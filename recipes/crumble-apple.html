Crumble Apple Recipe

description

Apple crumble is a dessert mostly prepared with stewed apples and topped with a crumbly crust. It is usually served along with cream, custard or ice cream.

Ingredients

Apple filling

  • 2lbs/1kg Granny Smith apples
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon

Topping

  • 1 cup rolled oats / oatmeal
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp cinnamon powder
  • 125g / 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • Pinch of salt

To Serve

  • Custard

Steps

  1. preheat oven to 180°C / 350°F.
  2. Peel apples, then cut into 1.5cm / 1/2" cubes.
  3. Place apples in a bowl. Sprinkles with flour, sugar and cinnamon, then pour over lemon juice. Toss, then spread out evenly in a 1.5 liter / 1.5 quart baking dish.
  4. Place topping incredients in a bowl. Mix until clumps form, like wet sand. Spread over the apples, crumbling with fingers if required to get thqt crumbly topping.
  5. Bake for 30 to 40 minutes or until golden brown. Remove, cover loosely with foil to keep warm and let stand for 10 minutes before serving.
  6. Serve warm with custard
